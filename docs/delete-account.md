# Request Account and Data Deletion

We're sorry to see you go! If you would like to delete your account, you can do so by sending an email request to our support team.

## What happens when I delete my account?
Upon processing your request, we will permanently delete your user account and all the content generated by you from our servers. This includes, but is not limited to:
- Your user account and profile data
- All your created recipes
- All your shopping list items
- All your to-do items and reminders
- All your calendar events

**Please note:** This data will be completely removed from all groups that you participated in. This action is irreversible.

## Send Deletion Request

Please send the deletion request from the email address linked to your account so we can verify your identity.
